Who we help

Built for every health goal

Whether your client is managing a chronic condition or working towards a lifestyle goal, Practice Health gives you and your client the tools to track progress and stay on course.

Diabetes & Metabolic Health

Track HbA1c, fasting glucose, and dietary compliance over time. Show clients how food and lifestyle choices directly move their numbers.

Weight Management

Log weight, body measurements, and meals in one place. Visualise progress week by week so clients stay motivated and on track with their goals.

PCOS & Hormonal Health

Monitor cycle patterns, weight fluctuations, and lab markers together. Correlate interventions with hormonal outcomes and build personalised plans that actually work.

Thyroid & Gut Health

Track thyroid markers, energy levels, and digestive symptoms alongside diet. Connect the dots between nutrition changes and how clients feel, every day.

Heart Health & Lipid Management

Log lipid panels, blood pressure, and dietary fat intake over time. Give clients a clear, visual story of how their nutrition programme is improving their cardiac risk profile.

General Wellness & Lifestyle

Not every client has a diagnosis — many just want to eat better, feel better, and build lasting habits. Practice Health keeps them accountable and you in control.
